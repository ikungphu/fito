About Fíto

As shoe retailers expand their selections of shoes in store and online, it has become increasingly more difficult for consumers to find shoes that fit them well.

We are partnering with Boogio to utilize smart sensors in the shoe to provide data via Bluetooth Low Energy. The beauty of such a sensor is that it is compact, inexpensive, and mobile. This is the feature of wearable sensors.



Fito is a dashboard that aggregates foot metrics collected through sensors in the shoe. The dashboard gives users feedback about their foot mechanics and provides recommendations regarding shoe fit, comfort, and support. These features empower the user to make informed decisions when buying new shoes.
